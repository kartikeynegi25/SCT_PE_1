# Prompt Examples

---

# Example 1

## Original Prompt

Explain climate change.

### Problems

- No audience
- No output format
- Too broad

---

## Improved Prompt

Explain climate change to a 12-year-old student.

Requirements:

- Maximum 5 bullet points
- Use simple English
- Give one real-life example
- End with one prevention tip

---

# Example 2

## Original Prompt

Write an email.

### Problems

- Missing recipient
- Missing tone
- Missing purpose

---

## Improved Prompt

Write a professional email to a client informing them that their website project will be delayed by two days.

Requirements:

- Professional tone
- 120–150 words
- Apologize politely
- Mention revised deadline

---

# Example 3

## Original Prompt

Tell me about Artificial Intelligence.

### Problems

- Too broad
- No audience
- No length

---

## Improved Prompt

Explain Artificial Intelligence to a first-year engineering student.

Requirements:

- Less than 250 words
- Use simple language
- Include three real-world examples
- Explain one limitation of AI
